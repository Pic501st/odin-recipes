Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

Description

These tasty chicken tacos can be made with less than 5 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 8 ounce bag shreeded cheese
  • 1 package of flour/corn torillas

Directions

  1. Combine the chicken broth and taco seasoning in a bowl
  2. Next, place the chicken in a slow cooker on low heat
  3. Cook chicken 4-5 hours (or until safe internal temperature)
  4. Shred your chicken and serve on tortillas with cheese
  5. Garnish with fresh salsa, tomatoes, sour cream or other favorite toppings!

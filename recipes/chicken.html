Slow Cooker Hawaiian Chicken

Description

This slow cooker meal is simple to prepare and contains few ingredients. This dish can easily be paired with white rice and steamed vegetables for a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken breast
  • 1 bottle of honey bbq sauce
  • 1 can sliced pineapple
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 2-3 cups white rice
  • 1 bag steamed vegetables

Directions

  1. Pan fry chicken on both sides until it begins to brown
  2. Place chicken in slow cooker with pineapple, bbq sauce, and soy sauce
  3. Cook on low heat for 4-5 hours (or until chicken reaches safe internal temperature)
  4. Prepare rice and steamed vegetables seperately
  5. Serve and enjoy!
